High Fiber Chia Seed Pudding Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for when you are craving something sweet and need to hit your fiber intake for the day

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes.
  • Preparation: 7 minutes.
  • Cooking 7 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 30 grams of chia seeds
  • 40 grams oats
  • 30 grams hemp seeds
  • Sweeter of choice (I prefer to use honey)
  • Fruit of choice (I often use frozen raspberries or fresh. They are high in fiber).
